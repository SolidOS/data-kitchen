Welcome to the Solid Data Kitchen!

Live your life in a read-write world of linked data! Use all your solid pods, including your local files as your zeroth pod. Bring in all the stuff in your life shared by friends and colleagues. Bring in public data like dbpedia and wikidata and the LOD cloud.

A context menu integrates the DataBrowser with file management and SPARQL querying and provides extra DataBrowser navigation. Right-click (ctrl-click for Mac) on any DataBrowser item or link.

Use SPARQL to query your own local files, pods, or any linked data source. Results are shown in a table. Clicking on a term in the table opens that term in the DataBrowser.

Copy, move, or delete files and folder trees between any spaces you have access to. Copy from pod to pod. Or between your pod and your local file system. Backup and restore. Save remote files locally.

Shortcuts save keystrokes, For example, @ldp:Container brings you to the LDP ontology entry for "Container".

Logging in is not needed for read/write of local resources or read of public resources. To write and visit protected resources, use the login button. When you are logged in, hovering over the logout button will show your webId.

See Help - Using the Kitchen for more details.
