Welcome to **Open Media Player**, a media organizer for public domain and freely available music on the <a href="archive.org">Internet Archive</a>.  This is a shared music space, you can listen to everything and create playlists for all to listen to.

The artists list is separated into curated at the top and raw at the bottom. Raw lists are based on dynamic searches and may contain random materials along with many gems.

## Quick Help :

### Listen to a track
  * select one or more playlists in the sidebar, double click on a track on the right
  * OR select a genre,  an artist, then an album, then double click on a track
  * Once tracks are loaded
    * use the play/pause or prev/next buttons
    * toggle random (button on upper right) to skip around within tracks in the tracklis
### View licensing and other information about the music
  * select the three dots to the right of an artist or track
  * select "visit on archive.org"
### Search for artists
  * use the "find an artist" input in the upper right
  * results (if any) will be shown in the album column, click on one to see tracks
### Create a playlist
  * select "+ playlist" at the bottom left
  * fill in and save the form that pops up
### Populate a playlist
  * find tracks by searching or by selecting from albums
  * highlight one or more of the tracks; drag & drop them onto the playlist
### Manage a playlist
  * drag & drop new items any time
  * use the three dots menu next to a playlist to edit or remove the playlist
  * use the three dots menu next to tracks to edit or remove tracks
### Resize Screen Sections
  * hover at bottom of the genres/artists/albums area, drag and move the boundary
  * OR hover at the right of the title or artist columnt, drag and move the boundaries
