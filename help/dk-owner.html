📰 News

  • Pick a topic, then a source, to read its latest stories; click an article to open it in a reader.
  • Rename a topic, drag a source between topics to re-file it, ＋ add a feed, or ✕ remove one. Use ⋮ → View deleted to restore.

🎵 Music & 🎬 Movies

  • Browse by genre / film-type → artist / collection → album → track, or search the Internet Archive and click to play.
  • In the music tracklist, 🎲 shuffles the play order and 🧹 clears the whole tracklist.
  • Keep listening with the mini-player while you visit other tabs.

🖼 Images

  • Drill Library → Topic → Collection, then scroll the grid; click a picture for the full-size view.
  • ＋ add your own topics and collections.

🗂 Workspaces

🗂 Podz & 🐧 SolidOS

amp; 🐧 SolidOS
  • Podz: sign in to a pod on either side and drag resources between panes.
  • SolidOS: the pod tree's Solid logo opens any resource in the data browser; your sign-in is shared into the browser pane.

★ Community Favorites

  • Tap ☆ to add a track, film, or collection — tap ★ again to remove your own.
  • As owner you can moderate: the ✕ in the Community Favorites list removes anyone's favourite from the shared wall.

🎨 Appearance & ⚙ Settings

  • Use the ☰ menu's Text size and Theme items for text size and light / dark.
  • ☰ → Customize ▸ Customize Preferences lists every editable widget; saves write back to the source data.
