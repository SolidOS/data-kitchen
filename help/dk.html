Welcome to data-kitchen — one shell, many plugins, one theme, one sign-in. You're browsing as a guest; everything here works without signing in.

🏠 Home

  • A dashboard: weather, clock, and a combined news feed.

📰 News

  • Pick a topic across the top, then a source below it to see its latest stories.
  • Click any article to open it in a reader.

🎵 Music & 🎬 Movies

  • Browse by genre / film-type → artist / collection → album → track.
  • Search the Internet Archive and click to play.
  • In the music tracklist, 🎲 shuffles the play order and 🧹 clears the whole tracklist.
  • Keep listening with the mini-player while you visit other tabs.

🖼 Images

  • Drill Library → Topic → Collection, then scroll the picture grid.
  • Click a picture for the full-size view — use ← → to page, click to zoom to 100%, or open it on Wikimedia Commons.

🗂 Data Kitchen Pod Browser & SolidOS

  • Data Kitchen Pod Browser: a two-pane Solid pod browser — sign in to a pod on either side and drag resources between them.
  • SolidOS: a pod tree on the left, the SolidOS data browser on the right; click the Solid logo next to a tree item to open it on the right.

🛠 Dev Tools

  • Solid reference links (website, FAQ, glossary, catalog) and developer playgrounds (JSON-LD, SHACL, SPARQL, an RDF validator) — each opens inside the tab.

★ Community Favorites

  • Tap the ☆ on a track, film, or image collection to add it to the shared Community Favorites — tap the ★ again to remove it.
  • It's a communal list: anyone can add and everyone sees it. Each tab shows its own Community Favorites list.

🎨 Appearance

  • Use the ☰ menu's Text size and Theme items for text size and light / dark.

Want to do more? Sign in (top-right) to manage your own library — organise news feeds, add image topics & collections, moderate the Community Favorites list, and install the app to your own Solid pod. Login also happens on demand: any save that needs sign-in opens the login picker for the duration of the flow.
