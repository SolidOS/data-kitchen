This Player provides access to thousands of free recordings of performances and broadcasts from the fabulous Internet Archive Live Music collections. It stores Linked Data which can be edited with a form supporting customized genres and artists. All materials are downloadable from the Internet Archive, please use the provided [IA] link to view licensing information on each item.
